About Me

I'm an aspiring web developer at Epicodus.
First heard about Epicodus while reading an article on wired.
At the time I was working as a database admin, after graduating as a software engineer, and I had lost track of the development world. When the opportunity presented itself I went for it only to find myself on the workbench of Epicodus.
And I couldn't be happier!

Background

I was born in Beirut, and raised in an open minded and supportive family who taught me to treat everyone with respect.
I spent most of my life in my home country Lebanon.
I have traveled to Cyprus, Turkey, Georgia, Italy, Bahrain, France, Greece, Belgium, California, and New York so far.
A few years ago I started riding and I fell in love with motorcycles and riding.
Now my main goal is to become a freelance web developer, buy a bike and tour the world.

Education

Where I come from people take education very seriously.
Then there's no wonder when I tell you that I went to one of the best private schools in Lebanon. After finishing school, I went to a private university where I majored in software engineering. and now after a while in the professional world (8 years) I decided it was time to go back to school and start a new career.

Experience

Brfore graduating I worked as a junior programmer in a company that created mobile and desktop application for warehouse management and salesforce for a couple of years.
After I graduated I worked for a year at a company specialized in banking web applications. At the time we were using XHTML, ajax and JSP to create our websites.
I only moved to the banking sector when I received a very generous offer from an 'A' bank in Lebanon. I worked there as a SQL database and reporting administrator for 6 years until I quit my job and joined this course.
